Welcome to Mile High Weights



Looking for quality weights but are off put by how expensive metal weights have become?


Did you know that prior to the COVID-19 global pandemic, about 95% of metal weights were produced in China?

Mile High Weights has an answer for you. We are selling competitively priced, and locally produced weights. We are selling 45, 25 and 10 lbs weight plates. Available in either Olympic or Standard barbell sizes

We produce the weights with a crack-resistant blend of concrete, composed of a proprietary mixture that includes reinforcing fibers and metal mesh to increase strength and ridigity

We start the process with a professional mold and our blend of crack resistant concrete and metal mesh


We then let the concrete cure for a week before painting


We then paint the plates according to the customer's wishes. Black seems to be the favorite, but you can customize!

Choose what you like.
